Why Table?

Table may seems easy, but is actually quite hard to get right. Table is useful for providing a rigid structure that help users visualize the information. However, table is not just about visuals. Semantics is crucial, giving meanings to the group of content matters especially for visual-impaired users.

The following is a table that labels every type of MBTI personalities.

MBTI
Sensing Intuition
Thinking Feeling Feeling Thinking
Introversion Judgment ISTJ ISFJ INFJ INTJ
Perception ISTP ISFP INFP INTP
Extroversion Perception ESTP ESFP ENFP ENTP
Judgment ESTJ ESFJ ENFJ ENTJ
