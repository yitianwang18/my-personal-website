Summary

This website is planned to be a personal portfolio site for people to learn about me. It is used to display my background, my experience, my projects, my hobbies, my personality and my contact information.

What are the visitors of my personal portfolio website?

People that are interested in learning about me. People like job recruiters, senior level engineers.

Recruiter Persona Diagram

Recruiter Goals

  • Recruiters are looking for people that are very suitable for the job the company needed and recruit them.

  • Contact the matching candidates.

  • Looking for candidates that displayed certain aspects of technical skills and domain knowledge. i.e. whether candidates know the knowledge of html, css, and javascript, web design, a11y, etc.

    • They may also care about foundations the candidate learned in school. Such as algorithms, data structures, design patterns, operating system, networking, security, etc.

  • Looking for applicants who exhibit certain traits that fit the company's culture.

    • Thought process when encountering a problem. e.g. habits of reviewing past projects and strive for the best. Rethinking and reviewing and learn from the experience.
    • How to handle stressful situation. Traits include personality, habits in the working, etc.
    • Teamwork, leadership, good heart. In general, learn about what kind of human being the candidate is.

  • Looking for applicants who displays potentials.

Pain points

  • Had a large poll of applicants.
  • Viewing portfolios can be very repetitive and tiresome.

How to make website accessible to them?

  • Search engine optimization so that they can find the website.
  • Make the website eye-refreshing and stands out.
  • Make the website easy to navigate and information easy to access.
  • Make the website performant.

Technical Requirements

I will host it on firebase.

Because most of the recruiter will likely be viewing my website in a web browser. I target most of the desktop browser. Mobile browsing support is a plus.

Since most recruiter will have reliable internet, I tried to target my page to be below 500kb in terms of size.

Because I have a form, I need to set up a server to receive the form information.

I plan to build a pong game using canvas and JavaScript.

I plan to explore the idea of svg and template and try to use JavaScript to create dynamic content and animations.
